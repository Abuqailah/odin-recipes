Grilled Chicken

charcoal grilled chicken

Seasoned and marinated chicken grilled over hot coals

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chick split
  • Honey sriracha seasoning
  • Olive oil
  • 2 whole oranges

Steps

  1. Butterfly chicken
  2. Mix olive oil and sriracha seasoning in freezer bag and marinate overnight
  3. Preheat grill to 400 degrees
  4. Place chicken on grill skin side up for 20 min
  5. Flip onto bone side for another 20 min
  6. Baste chicken on both sides using leftover seasoning from freezer bag while flipping every 5 min
  7. After chicken reaches 146 degrees internally remove and let cool before eating
