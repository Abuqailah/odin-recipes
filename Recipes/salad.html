Garden Salad

Garden salad made with fresh veggies

Garden salad with all he works

Ingredients

  • 1 head green leaf lettuce
  • 2roma tomatoes
  • 1 carrot
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan
  • organic mustard
  • 1/2 cup raw honey
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 red onion

Steps

  1. Chop lettuce
  2. Slice tomatoes
  3. Shred carrots
  4. Slice onion
  5. Mix mustard, olive oil and honey in small bowl and stir to desired consistency
  6. Mix all ingredients in salad bowl
  7. Drizzle dressing onto salad and enjoy
