Veggie Pizza

Homemade veggie pizza made from unique veggies

This pizza is made with gluten free flour and fresh green veggies.

The pasta sauce is made from fresh tomatoes and herbs and topped with fresh mozzarella and parmesan cheeses.

Ingredients

  • Fresh roma tomatoes
  • 1/2 lb fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 lb parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 lb fresh spinach
  • 1 whole onion
  • 1 clove garlic

Steps

  1. pre heat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Prepare dough for crust
  3. Mix tomatoes and herbs
  4. Shred cheeses into mixing bowl
  5. Chop spinach and mix with cheeses
  6. Spread sauce onto prepared crust
  7. Sprinkle cheese and spinach mix onto sauce and crust
  8. Bake in oven for 10 min then rotate and continue for another ten min
  9. Remove pizza and let sit for 5 min to cool
  10. Enjoy
